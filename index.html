About me

My name is Daniel and a Berlin native with a everchanging career like my beloved hometown.
I work as a photographer since the nineties, ran differents studios, became an UX Designer, and now I am a Full Stack Developer. Learing and trying new things out is in my DNA.
From early 2000s I explored CMS like Typo3, Wordpress and started building websites with plain code. As a photographer I transformed early into digital tools. My experience, especially with corporate customers, revealed the need for a streamlined tool. So, I decided to become an UX Designer and create an case study for a photo-proofing app.
Now as a Full-Stack-Developer I am realizing this vision.
